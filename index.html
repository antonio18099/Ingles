About the program

I chose this program because, ever since I was little, everything related to computers and technology has always caught my attention. I remember spending hours disassembling old electronic devices to understand their workings, which led me to self-learn about programming, hardware, and networks. My passion for technology has grown over time, and I am convinced that formal education in this field is the next logical step to achieve my professional goals.

ADSO, which stands for "Analysis and Development of Information Systems," is a program designed to train professionals in the field of information technology. We learn to analyze, design, develop, and implement information systems using various programming languages and technologies. In ADSO, I focus on acquiring skills in programming, systems analysis, and project management, participating in both theoretical and practical classes, and working on real projects that apply this knowledge.
